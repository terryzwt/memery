What is Microfiber Cleaning Cloth ?

Our cleaning cloth is made of ultra fine micro fiber which is the most advanced material and is quite different from conventional cleaner like a regular cleaning cloth or a tissue reducing transparency of the lens.

This textiles is developed through new technology making them even finer than silk.
Microfiber is a polyeter/nylon blend . The polyester and nylon are alternatively forced throught a nozzle that combines them through a heat seaving process . The fiber is then split a number of times to create the microfibers that are interwined to create an ultra absorbent microfiber material .

The thickness of micro fiber is finer than 1/100 of human hair and it is made of triangular section thread.
Our micro fiber cleaning cloth absorbs dust in itself efficiency and cleaning effect is excellent without any damage or scratch on the lens surface and you can maintain the better transparency of your lens. Due to the remarkable dust keeping ability, our micro fiber cleaning cloth protects the reverse sticking of dust to the surface of lenses.

 Why Microfiber ?

Our own specially designed microfiber cleaning cloth for high tech applications and for the cleaning of all lens surfaces

This Miracle Cloths cleans everything faster, easier, and better and saves you money; you can wash and reuse over and over and over again. It will provide you a pure, clean, simple, safe and fast method of cleaning.

Gentle to your glasses, mobile phone , camera , PDA , computer screens and delicate surfaces . Use the lint-free microfibre for polishing lenses and to eliminate dust and removing oil, dust, debris and fingerprints from camera lens or LCD screens. as the fibre is extremely fine and therefore it does not leave any scratches, mist , mark or fingerprints .
